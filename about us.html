OUR TIMELINE

2023

Introduced New Programmes

Bachelors of Data Science & Data Analytics,Bachelors of Economics & Political Science and Bachelors of Digital Communications & Project Management were introduced as General studies.

2021

Introduced New Programmes

BSc in Data Science and BSc in Statistics programmes were introduced in the Department of Mathematical Sciences.

2019

Introduced Single Degree Programmes

BA in Economics, BA in English, BA in Media Studies, BA in History and BSc in Geography programme were introduced as single subject degree programmes.

2017

Introduced New Programmes

BSc in Mathematics programme was introduced under the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science and BSc in Physics and BSc in Chemistry programmes were introduced under the Department of Physical Science. BA in Population and Develop ment Studies programme was introduced in the Department of Social Science as a single subject degree programme

2014

Introduced Two New Research Centres

Established two more new Centers of Excellence: Centre for Archeology and Historical Research (CAHR) and Centre for Science and Environmental Research (CSER).

2011

Established a New Research Centre

Established Centre for Climate Change and Spatial Information (CCCSI).

2009

Introduced Double Subject Degree Programmes

Introduction of BA in Political Science and Sociology, BA in History and Dzongkha and BA in Dzongkha and English as double subject degree programmes.

2008

Introduced New Programmes

BSc in Math and Physics, BSc in Math and Chemistry and BSc in Physics and chemistry were introduced. The College established the Center for Population and Development Studies (CPDS).

2006

Started Degree Programmes

Sherubtse College started offering degree programmes under the Royal University of Bhutan in Arts/ Science/ Commerce.

2004

Joined RUB

Sherubtse joins a host of other colleges to establish the Royal University of Bhutan.
